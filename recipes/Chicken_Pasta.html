Creamy Garlic Chicken Pasta

Enjoy a rich and comforting bowl of creamy garlic chicken pasta made with tender chicken, perfectly cooked pasta, and a smooth parmesan cream sauce. This easy dinner recipe is full of flavor and comes together quickly, making it perfect for busy weeknights or cozy family meals.

The combination of garlic, parmesan, and cream creates a luscious sauce that coats every bite of pasta and chicken. Whether you're looking for a new weeknight favorite or a delicious meal to impress your loved ones, this creamy garlic chicken pasta is sure to satisfy your cravings and become a staple in your recipe collection.

Ingredients

  • Pasta of your choice
  • Chicken breast
  • Olive oil
  • Butter
  • Garlic
  • Heavy cream
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Italian seasoning
  • Fresh parsley

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta according to package instructions and drain.
  2. Season the chicken with salt, pepper, and Italian seasoning.
  3. Heat olive oil in a pan and cook the chicken until golden and fully cooked.
  4. Remove the chicken and slice it into strips.
  5. In the same pan, melt butter and sauté the garlic until fragrant.
  6. Pour in the heavy cream and bring to a gentle simmer.
  7. Stir in Parmesan cheese until the sauce becomes smooth and creamy.
  8. Add the cooked pasta and toss to coat evenly.
  9. Return the chicken to the pan and mix everything together.
  10. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve warm.
