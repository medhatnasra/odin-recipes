Lasagna Recipe

Discover the comfort of homemade lasagna with layers of tender pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta, and perfectly melted cheese. This classic Italian-inspired dish is hearty, flavorful, and perfect for family dinners, special gatherings, or meal prep for the week ahead. Every bite delivers a delicious balance of savory sauce and cheesy goodness baked to golden perfection.

Whether you're making lasagna for the first time or looking to perfect your favorite recipe, this page will guide you through each simple step. From preparing the sauce to layering the ingredients and baking it just right, you’ll find everything you need to create a warm, satisfying meal everyone will love.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef or Italian sausage
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato sauce
  • Crushed tomatoes
  • Tomato paste
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Eggs
  • Olive oil
  • Italian seasoning
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Fresh parsley or basil

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions and drain them.
  3. Heat olive oil in a pan and sauté the onion and garlic until softened.
  4. Add the ground beef or sausage and cook until browned.
  5. Stir in the tomato sauce, crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, and Italian seasoning. Simmer for 15–20 minutes.
  6. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, eggs, and a little Parmesan cheese.
  7. Spread a layer of meat sauce in the baking dish.
  8. Add a layer of lasagna noodles over the sauce.
  9. Spread the ricotta mixture over the noodles, then sprinkle mozzarella cheese on top.
  10. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used.
  11. Finish with a final layer of sauce and a generous amount of mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  12. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  13. Remove the foil and bake for another 15–20 minutes until golden and bubbly.
  14. Let the lasagna rest for 10 minutes before slicing and serving.
